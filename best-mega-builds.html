Best Mega Kangaskhan, Metagross & Charizard Y Builds for Pokemon Champions

Apr 4, 2026By InciniForge8 min read

These three Mega Evolutions are the most popular and proven megas in Pokemon Champions. They've dominated VGC for years and they're back with a vengeance. Here's exactly how to build each one.

Mega Kangaskhan — The Turn 1 Bully

Type: Normal · Ability: Parental Bond · Win Rate: 49.1% · Usage: 14.2%

Why It's Elite

Parental Bond makes every move hit twice (second hit at 25% power). This means Fake Out does 125% damage with flinch, Power-Up Punch raises Attack TWICE per use, and Sucker Punch gets double priority damage. Mega Kangaskhan is the most oppressive lead in VGC history and it's back in Champions.

The Build

Nature: Jolly (+Spe / -SpA) or Adamant (+Atk / -SpA)
EVs: 4 HP / 252 Atk / 252 Spe
Item: Kangaskhanite
Moves:
  • Fake Out — Double-hit flinch. Devastating turn 1 pressure.
  • Return / Double-Edge — Main STAB. Return for consistency, Double-Edge for maximum damage.
  • Sucker Punch — Priority that hits Ghost-types super effectively. Double-hit means huge damage.
  • Power-Up Punch — +2 Attack after one use thanks to Parental Bond. One PuP and Kangaskhan sweeps.

Best Partners

  • Incineroar — Intimidate + Fake Out support while Kangaskhan Mega Evolves safely
  • Whimsicott — Prankster Tailwind so Kangaskhan outspeeds threats
  • Aegislash — Handles Fighting-types that threaten Kangaskhan

How to Counter It

Fighting-types (Lucario, Kommo-o) hit for super effective. Ghost-types are immune to Return/Double-Edge but watch out for Sucker Punch. Intimidate from Incineroar reduces its physical threat. Rocky Helmet punishes Parental Bond contact moves (triggers twice).

Mega Metagross — The Most Consistent Mega

Type: Steel/Psychic · Ability: Tough Claws · Win Rate: 50.1% · Usage: 16.7% (highest of any mega)

Why It's Elite

Mega Metagross is the most used Mega Evolution in Champions for good reason. Tough Claws boosts contact moves by 30%. With 145 Attack and 110 Speed, it outspeeds most of the meta while hitting incredibly hard. Steel/Psychic typing gives it 9 resistances and an immunity to Poison. Pre-mega, Clear Body blocks Intimidate.

The Build

Nature: Jolly (+Spe / -SpA)
EVs: 4 HP / 252 Atk / 252 Spe
Item: Metagrossite
Moves:
  • Iron Head — STAB + Tough Claws = devastating. 30% flinch chance is a bonus.
  • Zen Headbutt — Secondary STAB. Hits Fighting-types super effectively.
  • Ice Punch — Covers Dragon-types (Garchomp, Dragapult, Hydreigon) and Ground-types.
  • Protect — Essential in VGC doubles. Scouts attacks and stalls out field effects.

Best Partners

  • Incineroar — Handles Ghost and Dark types that threaten Metagross. Intimidate support.
  • Garchomp — Earthquake + Levitate-immune Metagross is a classic combo (Metagross isn't Levitate but resists Ground).
  • Whimsicott — Tailwind pushes Metagross's 110 Speed to insane levels.

How to Counter It

Fire-types (Incineroar, Arcanine) and Ground-types (Garchomp, Excadrill) hit for super effective. Ghost-types (Dragapult, Aegislash) hit its Psychic weakness while resisting Zen Headbutt. Bulky Water-types (Milotic, Dondozo) can tank its hits.

Mega Charizard Y — The Sun Sweeper

Type: Fire/Flying · Ability: Drought · Win Rate: 53.1% (highest WR of any mega)

Why It's Elite

Mega Charizard Y has the highest win rate of any Mega Evolution. Drought sets Sun on Mega Evolution, boosting Fire moves by 50% AND weakening Water moves against you. With 159 Special Attack, Sun-boosted Heat Wave in doubles does catastrophic spread damage. It also enables Chlorophyll partners (Venusaur) for a complete Sun team.

New mechanic: In Champions, Mega Evolution happens before switching. This means Charizard Y's Drought activates before the opponent can switch in a weather counter.

The Build

Nature: Timid (+Spe / -Atk) or Modest (+SpA / -Atk)
EVs: 4 HP / 252 SpA / 252 Spe
Item: Charizardite Y
Moves:
  • Heat Wave — Sun-boosted spread move. Hits both opponents for massive damage.
  • Solar Beam — No charge in Sun. Destroys Water, Ground, and Rock types that threaten you.
  • Overheat — Nuclear single-target nuke when you need to guarantee a KO. -2 SpA after.
  • Protect — Stall out opponent's weather or Tailwind turns.

Best Partners

  • Venusaur — Chlorophyll doubles Speed in Sun. Sleep Powder + Giga Drain.
  • Torkoal — Second Drought user for weather wars. Eruption under TR.
  • Incineroar — Intimidate + Fake Out while Charizard Mega Evolves.

How to Counter It

Rock-type moves deal 4x damage (Rock Slide, Stone Edge). Rain teams (Politoed, Pelipper) override Sun. Tyranitar changes weather AND resists Fire. Flash Fire users (Heatran, Arcanine) absorb Heat Wave.

Which Mega Should You Pick?

  • Mega Kangaskhan if you want maximum turn 1 pressure and a self-sufficient sweeper
  • Mega Metagross if you want the most versatile, consistent mega that fits on any team
  • Mega Charizard Y if you're building a Sun team or want the highest win rate
