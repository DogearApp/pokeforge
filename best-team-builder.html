Why InciniForge Is the Best Pokemon Champions Team Builder in 2026

Updated: April 4, 2026By InciniForge7 min read

Building a competitive Pokemon Champions team requires more than picking 6 Pokemon and hoping for the best. You need type coverage analysis, damage calculations, speed tier comparisons, EV optimization, and meta awareness — all working together. That's exactly what InciniForge was built for.

Here's everything InciniForge offers and why competitive VGC players are choosing it as their go-to team builder for Pokemon Champions.

100% Free, No Login Required

InciniForge is completely free. No accounts, no paywalls, no premium tiers. Every feature is available to everyone. Your teams save automatically to your browser — just open the site and your work is there. We believe competitive tools should be accessible to all players, whether you're a VGC veteran or building your first team.

Built Specifically for Pokemon Champions

Other team builders support all Pokemon games. InciniForge is laser-focused on Pokemon Champions. Toggle Champions mode and everything filters to the 150+ legal Pokemon. The search, tier list, threats analysis, meta data, and damage calculator all respect the Champions roster — no wading through 1,300 Pokemon to find what's legal.

Every Feature You Need in One Place

Smart Team Builder

Search any Champions-legal Pokemon with sprite previews showing base stats, types, and abilities before you add them. Advanced search lets you filter by move learned, ability, type, role (Physical Sweeper, Wall, etc.), or team comp (Trick Room, Sun, Rain, Sand, Tailwind). Drag to reorder your team, copy Pokemon between teams, manage multiple teams with notes.

Complete Move Selection with Usage Data

Every Pokemon's full move pool powered by Pokemon Showdown's learnset data — the most accurate source available, including pre-evolution moves that other tools miss. Each move shows its type, category (Physical/Special/Status), power, accuracy, PP, VGC usage percentage, targeting info (spread, hits ally, self), and full effect description. Moves are sorted by competitive popularity so the best options are always at the top.

VGC Usage Statistics

Real competitive data from Smogon's VGC 2025 stats (282 Pokemon) integrated throughout the builder. See usage percentages on every ability, item, nature, and move. Suggested EV spreads show the most popular competitive distributions — click to apply instantly. Know exactly what the top players are running.

Damage Calculator

Full Gen 9 Level 50 damage calculator built right into the builder. Select your attacker from your team, search for any opponent, and calculate damage ranges with:

  • Offensive buffs: Spread move, Critical hit, Burn, Tera, Helping Hand, Charge, Battery, Atk/SpA stages
  • Defensive buffs: Reflect, Light Screen, Aurora Veil, Friend Guard, Def/SpD stages
  • Weather and Terrain: Sun, Rain, Sand, Snow, Electric/Grassy/Psychic/Misty Terrain
  • Full item support: Choice Band/Specs, Life Orb, Expert Belt, Assault Vest, Eviolite, type-boosting items
  • Full opponent customization: Set any Nature, Ability, Item, EVs, and IVs on the opponent

Results show damage range, KO bracket (1HKO/2HKO/3HKO), HP bar visualization, and a full modifier breakdown.

Defensive Coverage Analysis

See your team's defensive weaknesses at a glance with a type matchup grid showing every type vs every team member (2x, 4x, resist, immune). Weakness bars show which types threaten the most members, with Pokemon sprites so you know exactly who's vulnerable. Separate ½x, ¼x, and immune columns. Critical weakness alerts warn when 3+ members share a weakness. Unresisted type alerts flag types nobody on your team resists.

Offensive Coverage Analysis

The flip side — see which defending types your team can't hit super effectively. Shows which team members cover each type with sprites and counts. Warns about single-point-of-failure coverage (only 1 member handles a type). Sorted worst-first so your biggest gaps are immediately visible.

Speed Tier Calculator

Compare your team's Speed against key meta benchmarks. Toggle Tailwind (2x), Choice Scarf (1.5x), Paralysis (0.25x), Icy Wind (-1 stage), and Trick Room (reverses order) to see exactly who outspeeds who under any condition. Modified speeds show the original crossed out next to the new value.

Common Threats Matchup Grid

A full grid checking your team against the top 30 VGC threats. Each cell shows whether your Pokemon resists, is neutral, is weak, or is immune to the threat's STAB moves — plus whether you can KO them back. Instantly spot holes like "nobody on my team handles Kingambit."

Team Analysis Engine

One-click analysis that checks everything:

  • Team archetype detection — identifies if you're running Tailwind, Trick Room, Sun, Rain, Sand, Hyper Offense, or Balance
  • Critical weaknesses and unresisted types
  • Offensive coverage gaps with specific move suggestions from your Pokemon's actual move pools
  • Speed tier analysis against common threats
  • Build validation — catches duplicate items (illegal in VGC), missing moves/items, low Protect usage
  • Doubles synergy checks — Fake Out, redirection, Intimidate, Helping Hand, Earthquake + Flying partner
  • Per-Pokemon suggestions — missing STAB moves, nature mismatches, EV issues
  • Pokemon recommendations to cover weaknesses with specific Champions-legal suggestions

Meta Insights

A dedicated Meta tab powered by Smogon VGC data showing:

  • What You'll Face — Top 15 Pokemon with their most popular ability, item, tera type, and moves
  • Popular Cores — Auto-detected common pairings from teammate data
  • Counter Suggestions — For each top threat, specific Pokemon that resist their STAB and hit them super effectively, with off-meta picks highlighted
  • Hidden Gems — Low-usage Pokemon that match up great against the current meta

Mega Evolution Support

Full support for all 80+ Mega Evolutions including every new Legends Z-A mega. Toggle Mega Evolution to see updated stats, types, abilities, and sprites pulled live from the API. Enforces the one-mega-per-team rule. Z-variants (Mega Absol Z, Mega Garchomp Z, Mega Lucario Z) and dual megas (Charizard X/Y, Mewtwo X/Y, Raichu X/Y) all supported.

Import, Export, and Share

  • Import — Paste any Pokemon Showdown team and it auto-builds everything (species, moves, EVs, IVs, nature, item, ability, tera)
  • Export — Generate Showdown-format paste with one click for use in Pokemon Showdown or in-game
  • Share via URL — Encode your entire team in a link. Send it to teammates, post it on Reddit, share on Discord — they click and see your exact team

Type Calculator

Quick standalone tool — select 1 or 2 types and instantly see what's super effective, what resists, what's immune, and what your STAB hits. No team needed. Perfect for quick "what beats Water/Fairy?" checks during team building or mid-tournament.

Usage Tier List

Browse the top 100 Pokemon ranked by VGC usage with sprites and usage percentages. Filtered by Champions mode. Click any Pokemon to add directly to your team. Mega-capable Pokemon are tagged.
